Dogs are the best!

Jackson

Jackson Sirius was my first dog. He was a Border Collie mix and he was a very good boy. He loved to go for walk, take float trips, and play fetch. He lived to be 14 1/2 years old.

Roxie

Roxie was the second dog I got. She is a Papillon and is currently almost 15 years old. She was a very smart dog in her prime and knew all sorts of tricks. These days she doesn't always understand what is going on around her, but she's still pretty healthy overall.

Stitch

Stitch was my Terrier mix who I found at 3 weeks old on a camping trip. He was separated from his parents and siblings too early and he had some issues because of it that were hard to work with. We made a lot of progress, but he ended up needing to find a new home when I had to move to an apartment.

Link

I got Link to be a friend for Stitch. He was a Miniature Pinscher mix who came from a shelter after spending the first half of his life living in a puppy mill. He also ended up being older than I thought, had clearly been abused, and took a lot of work to make him happy and healthy. He also went to a new home when I had to move into an apartment.

Katiebell

Katiebell is an American Bulldog mix who I took in as a foster after she and her litter were dumped in a box on a sidewalk in the middle of summer. She was immediately my soul dog and after I was told I would benefit from a service dog, she went to work with a trainer to become that for me. She is now 7 years old and has mostly been able to retire. She is still a very good girl who I adore and who loves to play, snuggle, and go on walks.

Noble

Noble was an American Staffordshire Terrier who I adopted from a shelter. He was a really loving dog who was super silly and loved to cuddle. Unfortunately he ended up having trouble with dog aggression, which was very difficult to manage living in a house with several other dogs. After my daughter was born, he was showing some red flags with her and I ultimately had to let him go. He'll always be my handsome, sweet boy, though.

Ocean

Ocean is a Bernese Mountain Dog and Poodle mix, otherwise known as a Bernedoodle. She is a silly girl who is all about snuggles. She also loves to play with her toys.
